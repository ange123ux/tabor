WHO ARE WE?

Tabor LTD is an organization that was founded by Benimana Jacques in 2002, with the aim of serving the customers to the fullest. TABOR LTD is a fast growing company that sells printers, laptops, stationaries, as well as consumables and spare parts of printers.

Tabor LTD provides services to its customers including maintainance, photocopy (full color and black color), printing, scanning, binding, laminating, and internet service

Tabor LTD focuses on the meaning, joy, and success of the lives of the customers through Quality, Rapidity and Effectiveness of the products and services provided.
